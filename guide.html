Plant Care Guide

Learn the best practices for watering, sunlight, pruning, and repotting your plants.

  • Water only when the top inch of soil is dry.
  • Place plants where they receive appropriate sunlight.
  • Trim dead leaves regularly to encourage growth.
  • Repot annually to provide fresh soil and more space.
